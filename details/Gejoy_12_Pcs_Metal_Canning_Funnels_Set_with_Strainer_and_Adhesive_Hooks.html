Effortless Kitchen Organization with the Gejoy 12-Piece Canning Funnel Set

The Gejoy 12-piece canning funnel set is a versatile addition to any kitchen, designed to simplify your day-to-day tasks. Made from high-quality 304 stainless steel, this set is built for durability and long-term use, withstanding the wear and tear of frequent kitchen activities.

Featuring two wide mouth mason jar funnels with comfortable handles, this set ensures safe and efficient pouring of hot liquids like soup, juice, or wine, without the risk of burns.
